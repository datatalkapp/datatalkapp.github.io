Introducing DataTalk: Revolutionizing Document Interaction and Business Intelligence

by Koen van Eijk & Thomas van Turnhout | January 8, 2024

Welcome to a new chapter in our journey as entrepreneurs and innovators. After our successful venture with Oakwood and the creation of Automagica, which found a new home with Netcall in 2020, we are thrilled to announce our latest venture: DataTalk.

DataTalk is not just a product; it's a vision of transforming how businesses interact with their everyday documents. We believe that your business documents, whether they are invoices, meeting notes, or audio recordings, hold a wealth of untapped insights. DataTalk is here to unlock this potential.

What Makes DataTalk Unique?

  • Transformative Document Interaction: Gone are the days of manually sifting through piles of documents. With DataTalk, you can ask questions and receive structured data outputs, making your documents as interactive and informative as a conversation with an expert.
  • Advanced Language Models: At the heart of DataTalk is the integration of sophisticated language models like ChatGPT. These models enable a deep understanding and processing of textual content, elevating the value of your documents.
  • Open-Source, Local Models for Privacy: Recognizing the sensitivity of business data, DataTalk offers the flexibility of local, open-source models. This feature ensures that your data remains within your control, bypassing the need for cloud uploads and offering unparalleled privacy.
  • Automated Data Processing Pipeline: Efficiency is key in business. DataTalk's sophisticated pipeline feature automates the processing of new data, seamlessly integrating with your existing systems and workflows.

As founders, our commitment to open-source principles, generative AI, large language models, and automation remains steadfast. DataTalk embodies these principles, offering a powerful tool that respects user privacy while delivering exceptional value.

Join Us on This Exciting Journey

We're inviting you to be part of this journey. Whether you're a small business owner, a manager in a large corporation, or simply an enthusiast of cutting-edge technology, DataTalk has something for you.
